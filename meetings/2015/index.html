Open XAL Presentations from CSNS Software Symposium - June 2015
CSNS held an ICFA mini-Workshop on Beam Commissioning June 8 to 10, 2015 at the China Spallation Neutron Source in Dongguan, Guangdong, China. Following this workshop was a two day symposium on software development for accelerators which featured Open XAL. Please note that these slides were presented as part of a live demo and not intended to be viewed standalone. They are included here for reference purposes only.
