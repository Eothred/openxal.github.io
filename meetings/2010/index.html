Open XAL Workshop - May 2010
The first Open XAL Workshop was held May 3 to 5, 2010 at SNS, Oak Ridge National Lab in Oak Ridge, TN. The primary goals were to review the current status of XAL and to develop a roadmap for active collaboration on Open XAL.
