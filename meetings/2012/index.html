Open XAL Workshop - December 2012
The second Open XAL Workshop was held December 13 to 14, 2012 at FRIB, Michigan State University in Lansing, MI. The primary goals were to provide a status report and discuss efforts to merge fragmented XAL development into a common Open XAL effort.
